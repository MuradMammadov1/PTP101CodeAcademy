    <h1>Homemade Apple Pie Recipe</h1>

    <h2>Ingredients:</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>6–8 medium-sized apples (peeled, cored, and sliced)</li>
      <li>1 cup granulated sugar</li>
      <li>2 tablespoons all-purpose flour</li>
      <li>1 teaspoon ground cinnamon</li>
      <li>1/4 teaspoon salt</li>
      <li>2 tablespoons butter (cut into small pieces)</li>
      <li>2 unbaked pie crusts (homemade or store-bought)</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>Instructions:</h2>
    <ol>
      <li>Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).</li>
      <li>In a large mixing bowl, combine the sliced apples, sugar, flour, cinnamon, and salt. Toss until the apples are evenly coated.</li>
      <li>Place one of the pie crusts into a 9-inch pie dish.</li>
      <li>Pour the apple mixture into the pie crust, spreading it out evenly.</li>
      <li>Dot the top of the apple mixture with small pieces of butter.</li>
      <li>Place the second pie crust over the apples and crimp the edges to seal the pie.</li>
      <li>Using a sharp knife, make a few small slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.</li>
      <li>Bake the pie in the preheated oven for 45–55 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.</li>
      <li>Remove the pie from the oven and allow it to cool for at least 30 minutes before serving.</li>
    </ol>

    <div class="serving">
      <strong>Serving Suggestion:</strong>  
      Serve your homemade apple pie warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a delicious treat!
